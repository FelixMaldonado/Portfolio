Portfolio

Quiz App

This app is a quiz based on a video game called Destiny. It asks the user ten questions based on the video game. If you get the answer correct, you will see your score along with a piece of lore relating to Destiny. If you get the answer wrong You will then see your score along with the correct answer. Once all questions are completed, you will be given the option to retake the quiz. This app was created using Javascript and JQuery.
